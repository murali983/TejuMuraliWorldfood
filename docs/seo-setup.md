## Production checklist
1. Connect Google Search Console.
2. Connect Bing Webmaster Tools.
3. Verify the canonical production domain.
4. Keep recipe pages long-form and non-repetitive.
5. Expand internal linking between related recipes, cuisines, and categories.
6. Review image alt text and media captions.
7. Track Core Web Vitals in production.
8. Avoid publishing large volumes of unreviewed AI text.

## EEAT guidance
